Girls Who Code at Georgia Tech is a club of female computer scientists passionate about empowering young women interested in STEM. Our goal is to facilitate the learning of young women who come to us by teaching as many coding concepts as possible through various exercises. Every weekend our activities are a choice of conversations about stem, highlight videos of incredibly empowering women, projects that strongly focus on computer science, and field trips to companies. We welcome young women from middle school to high school with any level of skillsets! Find more about Girls Who Code at their website.
